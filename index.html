Introduction (Working in progress)

I am an PhD student at the University of Genoa, Italy at the MaLGa lab, under the supervision of Nicoletta Noceti. I spent three months at IMAGINE computer vision team of École des Ponts ParisTech (ENPC) where I worked under the supervision of Gül Varol. My PhD topic is about understanding human motion from a computer vision perspective: minimal motion representation, and action prediction.
