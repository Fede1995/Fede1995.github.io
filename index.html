Introduction (Working in progress)

I am an Post-doc Researcher at the University of Genoa, Italy at the MaLGa lab, under the supervision of prof. Nicoletta Noceti .
My interests lie in developing algorithms for human motion understanding, scene graph for action recognition and weather prediction.

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher specialised in computer vision, with a focus on human motion representation and recognition. My PhD work involved significant contributions to these areas, including a project on motion anticipation.
Currently, I continue to advance research in human motion analysis while also expanding my expertise into weather forecasting in collaboration to the CNR (Centro Nazionale delle Ricerche), exploring the intersection of computer vision and meteorology.

During my Phd I worked on Skeleton data, image data and video data. Moreover I had the chanche to experiment with scene graph data for action recognitiona dn anticipation.
I also had the opportunity to spend three months at IMAGINE computer vision team of École des Ponts ParisTech (ENPC) where I worked under the supervision of Gül Varol.
My PhD thesis is about understanding human motion from a computer vision perspective: Understanding Humans in Videos: From Pose to Action Recognition
